Accompaniment


Susanna Valleau is an accomplished accompanist and performer. She has worked with soloists and musicians in many different settings — from recitals, festivals, and competitions to performances by chamber ensembles and orchestras. She is also on the faculty of the Seattle Girls' Choir as the piano accompanist for their senior choir.

To contact Susanna about accompaniment opportunities, please call 206.486.2801 or email susanna.valleau@gmail.com.
