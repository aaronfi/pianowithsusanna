Frequently Asked Questions


Where is the studio located?

In Susanna Valleau’s home in the Roosevelt neighborhood of Seattle, just south of Roosevelt High School.

How do I get started?

Contact us to arrange for a free trial lesson for you or your child. It’s a great opportunity for us to meet each other, work together, and decide if we're a good fit. Please call 206.486.2801 or email susanna.valleau@gmail.com.

What if I have to miss a lesson?

Students who cancel their lesson at least 24 hours in advance are put on a waiting list for a make-up lesson. Make-ups are scheduled in the order that cancellations occur. Unfortunately, we can’t offer make-up lessons with less than 24 hours notice.

What’s the time commitment for practicing?

For most students — adult and older children alike — practice sessions should run the length of their lesson time: 30 minutes if the lessons are 30 minutes, 45 minutes for 45-minute lessons; shorter sessions are better for younger children For additional guidelines, see Practice Tips.
